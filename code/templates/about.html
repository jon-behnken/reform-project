THE IDEA

The idea for Track The Change started with the recognition that our ability to effectively communicate with our legislative leaders is being frustrated by the transformations brought on by the digital age.

There are so many channels, platforms and social media services that many of us find it difficult to swim through the sea of usernames in an effort to be heard. Track The Change has a simple goal: provide a unified service that synthesizes the disparate contact information and methods for Congress men and women. As a little bonus, I've also added a basic bill searching tool that will show you recently drafted legislation so you can contact your representatives about something substantive should you feel inclined to do so.

THE DATA

Most of the legislative data such as bill numbers, bill text and history is sourced from ProPublica's Congress API.

The full text of a bill, when available, is retrieved from GovTrack's bill repository.

Contact information for legislators is sourced in large part with help from the Contact Congress project.
